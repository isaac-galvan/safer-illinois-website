Shield CU Family Testing Agreement Safer Illinois

The person named in the permission-request screen in Safer Illinois has self-identified as a member of your family or household (“Family Member”). They have requested access to the COVID-19 testing services provided by the University of Illinois ("University").

By touching the “AGREE” button in Safer Illinois, you agree that:

  • You are a University employee or student currently eligible for COVID-19 testing by the University;
  • Family Member is a member of your family or household; and
  • You are responsible for any unpaid fees incurred by Family Member in obtaining COVID-19 testing services from the University.

You also agree that you are accepting responsibility on behalf of the Family Member if the Family Member is a minor under age 18 and you are the parent or legal guardian of the Family Member.

You may revoke the Family Member’s access to University COVID-19 testing at any time through Safer Illinois, but you will remain responsible for any unpaid fees incurred by Family Member before the revocation takes effect.

If you believe the person named above is improperly requesting access and/or has improperly obtained your University Identification Number (UIN), please contact shieldcu@illinois.edu

For more information about the Shield CU family testing program, see SHIELD CU for Illinois Families.
